Clementine

A conference call and messaging app for enterprise · Acquired by Dropbox in 2015

I was tasked with creating a new icon set that would bring visual consistency throughout the app and instill a greater sense of trust in our customers.

Taking Stock

Several feature sprints at Clementine had resulted in icon bloat, with different styles of icons cobbled together from the Noun Project each time a new feature required them.

Revamping Clementine’s iconography started out as a simple project to soften and unify strokes on the existing app icons. I aimed for a new personable style aligned with our brand style. I chose three words that reflected Clementine’s product →

Clementine Brand Traits
